We are located in Franklin, Maine (Google Map), just east of Acadia National Park.

Our shop is stocked with Susanne’s hand-dyed wool and yarns, knitting, weaving and rugs. Charlie’s pottery includes gas-fired and wood-kiln-fired dinnerware, bowls, baking dishes, vases, mugs, pots, lamps, large planters and tiles.

The shop is kept neat from June to September, and is always available year ’round on a self-serve basis. Please call ahead (207-565-2282) if you want to be sure we will be here.
